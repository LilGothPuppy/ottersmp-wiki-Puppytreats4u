### Tutorial & Frequently Asked:

## 1. How do I get out of spawn?
- Do /rtp - this'll teleport you throughout our world to any non-claimed area. I recommend using this in tandem with /map to find a perfect housing spot.

## 2. How do I prevent my base from being griefed?
- Do /claimwand, use it by right clicking the floor once per corner to form a square/rectangular shape around whatever you are trying to claim. If you stop holding the wand, your attempt to claim will reset.

## 3. How do I get rich?
- For early game players, I recommend doing /missions to earn quick cash + keys that you can open or sell to other players. 
    - Farming & fishing are good for a bit of cash, but they're more repetitive. All require some work & prep though!
    - For more geared players, there is /mines, which I have separate guides for in [video showcase](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=M9o_oYA0i20).

## 4. How do I get back to my base if I want to leave?
- Make sure to /sethome before leaving your spot. Afterwards, use /home to tp back! You have 2 of them, so use them wisely.

## 5. How do I get cool custom items?
- There are a lot of ways to get custom stuff, some easy while others are not. 
    - For the free ways, /daily unlocks once a day and /milestones unlock while you're playing. You can also get cosmetics, gear, and more in /starfox. - Crates are also a great way to get gear, but that's entirely dependent on your luck.

## 6. How often does (x) update come out or (y) happen?
 - Seasonal updates, the big ones with spawn changes & all, usually release within the first week of every month. 
    - Another huge update drops each month on the 15th. Starfox changes every 2 months. 
    - A boss changes every month or two, with the older of the two bosses being replaced. 
    - Resource worlds reset either on the 15th or 30th. 
    - Leaderboards end on the 30th of every month.

## 7. Can I open my old keys?
- Nope, however you can use our NPC Sarah @ /warp crate_hall to convert 2 keys for 1 past seasonal key. (meaning 1 key of the crate that isn't currently the latest, but one month old) 

## 8. How can I buy and sell items?
- Players can use /ah and /shop to buy & sell items. 
    - On top of this, players can make their own shops @ /stalls and throughout the world by using /chestshop create! [See More](stalls.md)

## 9. What is there to do on Otter?
We have a ton of custom features and things to do, here are a few:
    - Run our many dungeon maps to gain loot and kill mobs/bosses
    - Collect tons of custom items, cosmetics and more
    - Gather every kind of custom fish that we have while experiencing our custom fishing plugin
    - Build a lovely town or home with our many pieces of furniture and custom blocks
    - Battle tough world-bosses throughout Otter to get rewarding loot
    - Interact with our lovely community & [make friends](https://discord.com/channels/1031357396301394022/1410753206597714032)
    - Become the owner of a wide variety of cute, custom plushies 
    - Grind for cash and kill some orcs in /mines

## 10. Is there anything on Otter that makes the survival experience easier?
- Besides the custom armor and items, we have two features intentionally made just to make your life easier. 
    - One of these is elevators, which can be purchased from players or crafted directly. (see the recipe in /recipes) For elevators, place down two on the same X/Z axis. If you jump on the lower one, which they can up to 30 blocks apart, it'll tp you to the higher one. This stacks infinitely, and you can go back down by shifting. 
    - The otherone is waystones, the popular mod, right click a waystone and it will register, allowing you to tp back with a different one! Visit a waystone @ /warp waystone.
