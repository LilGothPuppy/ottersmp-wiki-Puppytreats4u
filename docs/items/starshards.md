# Star Shards/Seasonal currency

Previously known as Star Shards, now known as seasonal currency are a form of common [currency](../Currency.md) found on OtterSMP.

The main use of seasonal currency are within the [Starfox](../vendors/starfox.md) shop.
## Obtaining Seasonal currency

Seasonal currency are a very easy thing to obtain. Because of this, prices found within [Starfox](../vendors/Starfox.md) tend to be expensive, some requiring up to over 1000 to purpchese items.

### Missions
The most popular and easist way to obtain seasonal currency, is found within the [/missions](../commands/basic.md) menu. Completing daily and weekly missions for each of our [NPCS]()to obtain seasonal currency and other items.

### Voting

The second most common method to obtaining seasonal currency is by voting, only downside is you can only vote for a server once a day. (You can vote on all 8 links once every 24hrs to obtain goodies from voting)

### Dungeons

The third common method of obtaining Seasonal currency is by repeatedly running [dungeons](../Dungeons.md) over and over again. While yes, this method may be repetitive, the amount of Star Shards gained makes it worth the time sink. On average, one can expect around a stack of Star Shards per dungeon run.

### Milestones


A not-so popular method of acquiring seasonal currency is by utilizing the [milestones](../Milestones.md) feature. The reason why I say this is such an unpopular route is due to the alternative rewards the player can choose instead. While yes, a lot of star shards are up for grabs if anyone really wants them, the other options include keys, armor sets, and more.
### Trading

Lastly, players can simply trade with other players to acquire seasonal currency. Utilizing things like the stall feature at spawn or simply peer-to-peer trading for items allows star shards to circulate throughout the world.
