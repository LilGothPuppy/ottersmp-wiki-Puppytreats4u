## Taylor the Quicksell Vendor

**Taylor** is the quick sell NPC vendor located at spawn next to Asuna and Luna. She can also be found in the Safari camp along side Carter and Tyler.

## Purpose

Taylor is a medium, similarly to Yuuki, Asuna, Luna, and Ella. She gives a friendly face to the quicksell menu. Players are able to click on Taylor to quickly sell any items that they place inside of the menu. She is unable to sell everything for you, but your able to see what items she is able to take along with their prices by accessing the shop menu with Asuna. 

Taylor will also buy three items at a higher price. The boosted items are randomly selected so be sure to check back often to see what she's looking for.


## Description

Taylor is a female NPC usually within the main building you appear either directly inside of, or right next to the entrance of, when you use the /spawn command in the Otter realm. She can be easily identified by the larger green and yellow text box above her head. 

Taylor has long auburn locks that cascade over her right shoulder and green eyes. Her dress is comprised of a long sleeved off the shoulder top, a corset, and a teal fabric bottom overlay. 


## Trivia

- Taylor is one of the few physical NPCs that doesn't have an item or cosmetic on them.
